Cancellation and Refunds Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Wellness Balance's Cancellation and Refunds Policy. This page explains how you can cancel your order and request a refund, as well as our return and exchange policies.

Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your order within 24 hours of placing it. To request a cancellation, please contact our customer support team at support@wellnessbalance.com. Note that certain products or services may not be eligible for cancellation.

Return Policy

Items can be returned within 30 days of receipt. Please ensure that the items are unused and in their original packaging. Return shipping costs may apply. To initiate a return, contact customer support for instructions.

Exchange Policy

Exchanges are available for defective or incorrect items. To request an exchange, follow the return process and specify the item you wish to receive in exchange. Exchanges are subject to availability.
