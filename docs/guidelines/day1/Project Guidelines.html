E-Commerce Project Guidelines

Teams have been assigned to create a balance of skill and interests. Follow these guidelines throughout the project.

Team guidelines

  1. Assign work to individuals according to their abilities.

  2. Assign at least some programming tasks to every team member.

  3. Provide support/mentoring to each other as required.

  4. Break work into the smallest possible components so that it can be shared equally.

  5. Review pull requests quickly and merge them into main branch when ready... CI.

Individual guidelines

  1. Every day, seek out work that you can do; your work should not be restricted to your 'role'.

  2. Commit your work frequently and make pull requests daily. Your branch should contain your name. eg: navdeep_books_edit_form

  3. Don't take on more work than your share; allow other team members to earn marks.

  4. Treat the course like a job. Attendance is taken at 9:00 AM or at a previously specified time. You should be in attendance for at least three hours daily.

  5. Keep your time sheet current; it is reviewed during the project manager meeting, and should reflect your git log. (please track time for meetings, mentoring, research, etc.) Timesheets should be as granular as possible.
