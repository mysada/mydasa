Marks

How marks are awarded

As per the course outline, project marks themselves are split between the deliverable components.

The completed eCommerce project itself is worth 40% of your final mark for the project course.  Each team member earns the same mark for this component.

The other 60% of your final mark is based entirely on your personal contribution to the project.

Please see the breakdown below:

Group Marks (worth 40% of full mark)

All team members earn the same group mark. It is based entirely on the work submitted.

  • Configured Server - marked on day 1
  • Graphic Design - marked on day 2 (feedback will be provided)
  • HTML/CSS Templates (Front End) - marked on day 3 (feedback will be provided)
  • Proposal - marked on day 3
  • Completed Site  - marked on day 12

Individual Marks (worthy 60% of full mark)

Individual marks are based on a variety of factors; marks are awarded or deducted daily on a pro-rated basis.

  • Professionalism/Timesheets  - Timeliness, participation, granularity of time entries, completeness of timesheet - marked daily
  • Git record - contributed work (merged pull requests) and log of that work - marked daily
  • Presentation  - Language, grammar, tone, completeness - marked on day 12
